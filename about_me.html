My Favorite Restuarant:

This place has some of the most delicious ramen around! Easily some of the most flavorful broth in the city, with an affordable price to match. If you want to see more just look at their menu here.

My Favorite Food:

I love ramen. There are so many different kinds of broth, but I adore a pork or miso broth. Tonkotsue or miso ramen is easily the best, and if you too enjoy this wonderful food check out the link above!
